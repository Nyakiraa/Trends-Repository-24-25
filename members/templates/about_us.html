About Us

The Ateneo Payment System is an innovative digital platform designed to streamline financial transactions at Ateneo de Naga University. Developed by students from the Department of Computer Studies, this system aims to enhance the efficiency, security, and accessibility of payment processes for students, guardians, and the Treasurer's Office.

For years, the university has faced challenges such as long queues, delayed payment reflections, and verification issues, especially during peak enrollment and examination periods. The Ateneo Payment System addresses these concerns by offering multiple payment options, including online, offsite, and over-the-counter transactions. With features like real-time payment verification, automated receipt generation, and a digital queuing system, the platform ensures a seamless and hassle-free experience.
